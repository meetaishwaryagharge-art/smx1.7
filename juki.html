About Juki

Japan's No.1 Industrial
Sewing Machine Brand

Juki Corporation, founded in 1938 in Japan, is the world's leading manufacturer of industrial sewing machines. Trusted by garment factories across 170+ countries, Juki machines are synonymous with speed, reliability, and precision stitch quality.

SMX India is a proud distributor of Juki machines in Mumbai and across India, offering the full range from high-speed lockstitch to specialized overlock, bartack, and computerized machines.
